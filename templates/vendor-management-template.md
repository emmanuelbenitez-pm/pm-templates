## ğŸ’¡ Best Practices

### Vendor Management Do's âœ…

1. **Set Clear Expectations** - Document everything in SOW
2. **Communicate Regularly** - Weekly check-ins minimum
3. **Track Performance** - Measure against SLAs
4. **Build Relationships** - Vendors are partners, not just suppliers
5. **Pay On Time** - Builds goodwill and trust
6. **Provide Feedback** - Help them improve
7. **Plan for Transitions** - Have exit strategy
8. **Document Everything** - All agreements in writing

### Vendor Management Don'ts âŒ

1. **Don't Skip Due Diligence** - Always check references
2. **Don't Ignore Red Flags** - Trust your instincts
3. **Don't Over-Depend** - Have alternatives ready
4. **Don't Micromanage** - Trust but verify
5. **Don't Delay Payments** - Hurts relationship
6. **Don't Skip Contracts** - Verbal agreements fail
7. **Don't Forget to Review** - Regular performance reviews
8. **Don't Burn Bridges** - May need them again
