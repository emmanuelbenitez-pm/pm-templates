## ðŸ’¡ How to Use This Template

### Frequency Options
- **Weekly:** Standard for most projects (recommended)
- **Bi-weekly:** For slower-moving or smaller projects
- **Daily:** Crisis mode or go-live periods

### Customization Tips
1. **For Executives:** Keep to 1-2 pages, focus on Executive Summary & Decisions Needed
2. **For Team:** Include more detail in Progress Metrics & Activities
3. **For Agile Projects:** Emphasize sprint metrics, burndown, velocity
4. **For Waterfall:** Emphasize phase completion, milestone dates, gate criteria

### Best Practices
- âœ… **Be concise** - use bullet points, not paragraphs
- âœ… **Be visual** - use colors, emojis, progress bars
- âœ… **Be honest** - don't hide problems, propose solutions
- âœ… **Be consistent** - same format, same day, same time
- âœ… **Be actionable** - every red item has an action plan
- âœ… **Update metrics** - don't copy/paste from last week

### Red Flags to Avoid
- âŒ No clear status (green/yellow/red)
- âŒ Burying critical issues in middle of document
- âŒ Vague language ("making progress", "almost done")
- âŒ No actions for problems identified
- âŒ Inconsistent format week-to-week
- âŒ Too long (>3 pages for executives)
