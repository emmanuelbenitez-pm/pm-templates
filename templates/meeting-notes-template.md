## ğŸ’¡ Meeting Best Practices

### Before the Meeting

**Organizer Checklist:**
- [ ] Clear agenda sent 24 hours in advance
- [ ] Required attendees confirmed
- [ ] Pre-reads shared (if applicable)
- [ ] Meeting room/virtual link tested
- [ ] Time zone confirmed for remote attendees

### During the Meeting

**Facilitator Responsibilities:**
- âœ… Start on time (5-minute grace maximum)
- âœ… Review agenda and objectives
- âœ… Keep discussion on track
- âœ… Timebox agenda items
- âœ… Capture decisions and action items
- âœ… Park off-topic discussions
- âœ… End on time or early

**Participant Responsibilities:**
- âœ… Come prepared (read pre-reads)
- âœ… Actively participate
- âœ… Stay focused (no multitasking)
- âœ… Respect speaking time
- âœ… Commit to action items

### After the Meeting

**Note Taker Checklist:**
- [ ] Publish notes within 24 hours
- [ ] Send to all attendees + relevant stakeholders
- [ ] Update action item tracker
- [ ] Log decisions in decision log
- [ ] Schedule follow-up meetings if needed

## âš ï¸ Meeting Anti-Patterns to Avoid

### Poor Meeting Practices

âŒ **No Agenda**
- Meetings wander without direction
- Time wasted on tangents
- Nothing gets accomplished

âŒ **Wrong Attendees**
- Decision makers not present
- Too many observers
- Missing key stakeholders

âŒ **No Action Items**
- Discussion without outcomes
- Nothing happens after meeting
- Same topics every meeting

âŒ **Status Updates Only**
- Could have been an email
- No decisions or collaboration
- Waste of everyone's time

âŒ **Recurring Meeting Zombies**
- Meeting continues even though not needed
- "We've always had this meeting"
- No one remembers original purpose
