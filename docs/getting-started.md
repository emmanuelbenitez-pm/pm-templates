### For Your First Project

**Start with these 5 essential templates:**

1. **Project Charter** - Define your project

**Large Project (9+ months, 15+ people):**
- Use all templates as needed

## âœï¸ Step 3: Customize Templates

### Basic Customization (10-15 minutes per template)

**Every template has sections you can customize:**

1. **Document Information**
   - Fill in project name, dates, owners
   - Update version numbers

2. **Remove Unused Sections**
   - Delete sections not relevant to your project
   - Keep templates lean and focused

3. **Add Your Branding**
   - Add company logo
   - Update headers/footers
   - Adjust color schemes (if applicable)

4. **Adapt Examples**
   - Replace example text with your project specifics
   - Update sample data in tables

---

### Example: Customizing Project Charter

**Original template has:**
- [ ] Project name: [Enter project name]
- [ ] Business sponsor: [Name]
- [ ] Project manager: [Name]

**Your customized version:**
- [x] Project name: Customer Portal Redesign
- [x] Business sponsor: Jane Smith, VP Marketing
- [x] Project manager: Your Name

**Time to customize:** ~30 minutes  
**Result:** Professional project charter ready for approval

## ðŸ’¡ Tips for Success

### Do's âœ…

2. **Customize Immediately** - Don't use templates as-is
3. **Remove Unused Sections** - Keep templates lean

## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to use all 29 templates?**  
A: No! Use only what adds value to your project. Small projects might need 4-6 templates, large projects 15-20.

**Q: Can I modify the templates?**  
A: Absolutely! Templates are meant to be customized to your needs.

**Q: What format are the templates in?**  
A: Markdown (.md) format - easily convertible to Word, PDF, or HTML.

**Q: How long does it take to fill out a template?**  
A: 15-30 minutes for simple templates, 1-2 hours for complex ones like BRD or Business Case.

**Q: Can I use these templates commercially?**  
A: Yes! MIT License allows free commercial use.
